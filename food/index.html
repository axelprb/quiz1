Traditional Food

Gado-gado

A classic Indonesian salad featuring a mix of boiled vegetables, tofu, and rice cake, all served under a rich and savory peanut sauce dressing.

Kerak Telor

A legendary Betawi snack, this is a savory and crispy omelette made from glutinous rice and duck egg, cooked over charcoal and topped with spiced coconut flakes.

Ketoprak

A popular street food dish consisting of rice cake, tofu, rice vermicelli, and bean sprouts, all brought together with a savory peanut sauce freshly ground with garlic.

Soto Betawi

A traditional beef soup from Jakarta, characterized by its uniquely rich and creamy broth made from a combination of coconut milk and fresh milk.
